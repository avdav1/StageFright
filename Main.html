Stage Fright: Your new

favorite indie rock band.

We are an indie rock band out of Spokane, Washington. Our purpose is to spread

awareness for social anxiety through music and create a space where everyone

can share what they love without judgement.

With our band Stage Fright we are striving to create safe spaces where

everyone is free to express themselves through music.

It can be super scary to connect with others. When we first starting performing out of a Washington

amusement park, we were terrified of sharing our love of music with others. Music is something

that is very important to us and we were afraid of external judgement. Eventually with time and support,

sharing what we loved became easier and easier. Because of this experience we want to bring awareness

to social anxiety through our music.
